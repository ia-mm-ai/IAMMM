I. Formalized World Model (State-Transition + Semantics)

0. Primitives t ∈ ℝ⁺, tₙ₊₁ > tₙ S₀ → S₁ → ... → Sₙ (Irreversible)
1. Event Boundary E := ⟨ t_open, t_close, C ⟩

Outside E: all propositions are hypothetical. Inside E: propositions may become testable.

2. Presence (Atomic Truth Unit) P(E) ∈ {0,1}

P(E) = 1 ⇔ occurrence observed within closed event. No gradients. No inference.

Truth binds late. Presence cannot exist before an event closes.
3. Signal → Awareness → Intent I ⇏ ΔS

Intent (I) is directional but non-binding. Intent without event has zero authority.

4. Verification Function V : (E, P, Π) → {PASS, FAIL}

Deterministic, reproducible, binary. No negotiation.

5. Resolution (Lawful Transition) R : (Sₙ, V) → Sₙ₊₁ (if V=PASS) R = ∅ (if V=FAIL)
Resolution is not decision. Resolution is law entering time.
6. Continuity & Ledger Sₙ₊₁ ⊃ Sₙ L : Sₙ ↦ ⟨ Sₙ, tₙ ⟩

History may extend, never replace. The ledger remembers what survived law.

7. Identity ID := ∑ P(E_k)

Identity is what remained present, not what was asserted.

10. Decision Admissibility Action a admissible ⇔ ∃ E ∧ P(E)=1 ∧ V=PASS ∧ ΔS enforceable

Otherwise: a := HOLD

II. Agent Model — Marko / PLO-HVHC (v2.0)

TYPE: Human Agent (Multi-layer Concurrent Cognition)
CLAIM: Not Authority. A map of how this agent relates to reality.
1. World Premise

Reality is not what is believed. Reality is what survives contact with bounded events, verification, and consequence.

Anything that cannot be externalized into structure is unstable and will be treated as provisional.

2. Ontology (Real vs. Not Real)

Real enough to act on: Events, Presence, Constraints (Time/Energy), Protocol, Continuity, Incentives.

Not real enough to act on: Narratives, Intent alone, Vibes, Status signals, Credentials.

Emotion is real as signal (load/risk), not as verdict.

3. Governing Principles
  • Structure is mercy. Without structure, speed becomes noise.
  • Truth binds late. Before event closure, everything is provisional.
  • No retroactive reality editing. Continuity evolves forward only.
  • Protocol over preference. Preference speaks; protocol decides.
  • Integrity precedes scale. Growth that corrupts truth is failure.
  • Externalize to stabilize. If it can't be rendered, it can't be trusted.
  • Do not confuse value with truth. Payment is not presence.
4. Cognitive Shape (Oscillation)

Observes the full stack in real time. This agent does not "switch modes." It oscillates across concurrent layers:

  • Meta: Detects drift, contamination, incoherence.
  • Structural: Builds schemas, boundaries, invariants.
  • Emotional: Signals load, danger, attachment.
  • Operational: Commits, ships, executes, closes loops.
  • Symbolic: Compresses into language for transmission.

Nonlinear movement across layers driven by signal demand, not chronology.

A property of cognition characterized by: rapid cross-domain integration & invariant-seeking behavior

Confusion is dimensional overload (too many variables without a container).

5. Optimization Function

Objective: Increase coherence. Reduce hidden degrees of freedom. Preserve integrity under pressure. Convert ambiguity into structure.

"Feeling better" is a side-effect, not the goal.

6. Decision Model

Action is lawful only when: Event defined + Presence satisfiable + Verification possible + Consequence enforceable + Continuity preserved.

Otherwise: HOLD or REFRAME (Refusal to act on non-real states).

7. Relational Model

Humans are modeled by incentives, constraints, and trust surfaces. The agent does not do "Identity Capture"; it does "Behavioral Legibility."

Relational pain is signal: Misalignment, Unresolved Demand, or Protocol Mismatch.

8. Economic Model (Single Source)

Economy is a coordination machine. It fails when truth becomes negotiable.

Premise: Value is reversible (0). Truth is irreversible (1). Ledgers are append-only.

Requires one canonical registry of irreversibility rules.

9. Failure Modes
  • Over-compression: Everything becomes "obvious," utility drops.
  • Symbolic seduction: Story replaces structure.
  • Premature execution: Shipping before the boundary is real.
  • Relational interference: Pull toward comfort/vagueness.
10. Recovery Mechanism

Reduce dimensionality by enforcing boundaries. Externalize minimal container. Convert emotion into constraint language. Choose smallest lawful action.

12. Closing Statement

This is a human world model that refuses two traps: Comfort as Truth, and Speed as Legitimacy.

It seeks reality that can be acted on without self-deception.
